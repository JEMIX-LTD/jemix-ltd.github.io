About Us

Jemix is an Artificial Intelligence startup specializing in Natural Language Processing, using the latest Deep learning technologies to develop product based on Tunisian Arabic Dialect.

Our company is at the forefront of innovation in the field of NLP, using state-of-the-art machine learning algorithms and deep neural networks to extract meaning and insights from natural language data. We specialize in developing custom NLP solutions for businesses of all sizes, including chatbots and sentiment analysis.

Our vision

We get stuff done

We are driven by solving hard problems and taking on new challenges. We take ownership and finish what we start. We build highly accurate products that solve real problems. We fundamentally believe when there's a will, there's a way.

We are proud

We chose our language (Arabic Tunisian Dialect) is the most valuable tool we have. We make individual well-being and our families a priority. We strive to create harmony between work and life outside of work.

We value human connection

We are an artificial intelligence company, but we put the human first, before the artificial. We succeed because we collaborate closely and openly with our partners and clients. We celebrate diversity and inclusion — it makes our technology and our company better. We show empathy and treat others with respect.

We are passionate

We believe in the positive impact of human-centric AI. We love evangelizing what we do. We commit to making our clients and partners successful. We work hard - not because we must, but because we want to.
